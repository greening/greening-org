Notes:

    To personalize a web site or web catalog, you can use LikeMinds WebSell. WebSell is a “collaborative filter”, which means it helps you find the items best for you by looking at what other people like you liked.
    WebSell stores consumer preferences, behaviors, and purchases in a database, or can use your existing database. When asked to personalize a page for you, it finds people like you in the database (relations), then finds what they responded to, then predicts what you will like and recommends those.
    WebSell provides customer specific product recommendations, predicted satisfaction, and products for two or more.
    What can you do with that information? You can highlight products from a catalog that a customer will be more likely to buy. You can order web pages in a menu in the order most appealing to the visitor. You can suggest products that will satisfy a whole family or a couple.
    Finally, WebSell can create groups of likeminded consumers. That allows you to market products to a whole group, in a chat area for example. The products being marketed are a cue for what the group shares in common. So what do you think they’ll use as the icebreaker?
