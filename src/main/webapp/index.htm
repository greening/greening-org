Dan R. Greening, Ph.D.
Dan Greening at Christo's "The Gates"

One-sentence summary: I'm a gregarious, outdoor-sports loving, art-appreciating, politically-savvy computer scientist, entrepreneur and writer.

I like helping to organizing people, teams, departments and companies to more effectively produce value. Creating highly productive groups through reorganization has been a driving theme in my life for over 20 years.

I currently work for Citrix Online as Director, Agile Program Office. I work with the heads of Engineering and Product Management to optimize developer effort, maximize value and agilely identify and respond to new markets.

I have been involved in several software startups. Some gained liquidity, one did not. I also keep a toe in academia: I was Principal Investigator on three National Science Foundation grants.

I love to travel. This photo has a story: Overstock.com's CEO had a meeting in New York. Happy with our efforts to restructure his engineering department, he took my collaborator/husband Ron Lussier and me along in his jet (wow), and put us in a hotel facing Central Park. The snow was falling on Christo's Gates.

When profession, recreation and creativity flow seamlessly, when I can't distinguish work from fun, that's when life is good.
