angst in hot sun atoms burning life

boiling sphere of tension
scream for me as I crawl
the path so jagged and thin.
empty howl of delight perverse
last opera of noise pulls me forward.

and the sound too becomes
thin like a manuscript
gaunt like a man on his last diseased legs
to disappear in heaven lost
falling in the seething pool

scream.

yes like a baby unknowing
you ask that question
that grates my barricaded soul
peelings of soft flesh dropping
what is life? what is life?

walking the mountain cliff, a bird
a fly, a brain coiled snake hiss
in quiet oh so quiet calm
a bell
pure tones

harmonics oh so pure.

stand on the edge watching
gasping air, trees rooted clutching
at existence rarified
at rocks wanting a release moment
so orgasmic to fall but no

scream.

in the tension that brings the teeth
to the bone
scraping life from the bottom
from the hidden recesses
your sharpened nails cut

once you left me an aftertaste blood delicate
the existential dream
as in a morning fog
burned by fire so hot
charred and I asked you to forgive

ignored in the voices all begging
with tongues outstretched
``feed me please just a drop
essence of life now in infinite mercy''
and I walk on ashamed

consoling hand reaches through heaven
grasping mine
draw me closer Sol
my atom maker smasher
radiating windy fearless plumage

you strip me naked like a bone
dry and baked on a salty shore
burn me barren cracking reckless idiocy existence
you shatter me with a hammer

I flourish on this ascetic path
worthless and dying
while the world turns
the wind wails
and Sysyphus' rock falls crashing again

if all motion stops
if the world finds peace
a vacuum
so pure
then love howls in a black hole silent

scream.

join the universe in song
and intone the name of God.
