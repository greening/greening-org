Notes:

    Here’s the prediction screen I got when I clicked on “Sling Blade”. Moralistic me got the prediction “Loved It” for Sling Blade, two points higher than the average person in the system.
    If you look down below, you see a target with an arrow. There are three rings and one bullseye in the target. In this example, the arrow is in the third ring, very close to “on target”, which would be in the bullseye.
    LikeMinds is very interested in accurate predictions. We have a system that allows us to determine how accurate our predictions are, by using your ratings as sample points.
    MovieCritic has a 13 point scale, with “Loved It” being 13 and “Hated It” being 1. When the arrow is in the bullseye, our prediction is within 1 point of your actual rating 90% of the time. In other words, our high confidence predictions are within 7% of actual 90% of the time. Pretty darn good.
    It keeps getting better, because we are continually testing and refining our methods.
