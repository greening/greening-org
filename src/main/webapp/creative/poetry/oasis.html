oasis

on this shore, an outsider's mind
western water, shallow and blank

full stop
no movement
bright red brakes

los angeles interstate 405, it is raining

reflections dancing, mass media's showers
automobiles rumbling, transit's breaking waves
mass engineering glass, society's cold black emptiness

sharp edges tense on wet cement

then the still waters became a maelstrom
and that mirage oasis became a screaming hoover.
