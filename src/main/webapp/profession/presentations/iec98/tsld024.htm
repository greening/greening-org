Personalization Benefits

  • Help customers find desired products

  • Gain more repeat traffic

  • Reduce price competition

  • Raise data barriers to competitors

  • More loyal customers (“my catalog”)

Notes:

    So let’s get to the point, at least my point. There are lots of benefits to personalizing your marketing efforts online.
    By offering products better suited to each individual consumer, you’re more likely to get them to buy from you. Hence, more electronic commerce for you at not a very high cost.
    Personalization is service, and consumers pay for service. Why compete on margin? Instead, give consumers a great shopping experience, then they buy from you even if it costs a little more.
    Finally, when you give personalized service to your customers, they become more loyal to you. I bought my last suit from Ken Kaiserman at Nordstrom, and I’ll buy my next one from him too. He knows I like modern suits that I can squash into luggage, he remembers my name, he suggests great shoes. I’m a loyal customer.
