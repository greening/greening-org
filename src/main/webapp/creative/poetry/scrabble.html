scrabble

two people playing scrabble
smiling and chatting
a park bench. trees. grass.

the man places a D on the board.

the woman frowns and secretly ponders
her next move. will she
take him to bed? get her country home?

she forcefully snaps an E.

he comments on her acumen
hoping for light fingers brushing his chest
