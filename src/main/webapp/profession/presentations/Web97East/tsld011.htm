Notes:

    To show these concepts in action, let’s talk about MovieCritic, the LikeMinds demonstration site.
    MovieCritic suggests movies specific to your taste, not the taste of the average person. Here’s how it works.
    You rate some movies, and WebSell stores those movie ratings in a database.
    WebSell finds the people who best share your taste in movies: you liked Die Hard, they like Die Hard, you hated Total Recall, they hated Total Recall. It then weights each other person (we call these mentors), with a higher weight for people more like you.
    To predict your interest in different movie, WebSell takes the people most like you and use their ratings as predictors for your ratings.
    To provide recommendations, WebSell sorts the movies in order of how well it thinks you’ll like the movie. MovieCritic presents those movies in the Best Bets list.
    Based on the strength of your relationship with your mentors, and the controversiality of a movie (or product, etc.) we can provide a “confidence level” for each prediction.
