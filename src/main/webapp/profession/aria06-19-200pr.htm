MACROMEDIA DELIVERS ENHANCED WEB SITE ANALYSIS WITH ARIA ENTERPRISE 5.0
Analysis solution expands reporting and scalability, introduces Oracle 8i platform

Macromedia Web World, Seattle, Washington —June 19, 2000—Macromedia, Inc. (NASDAQ: MACR)), the leading provider of solutions for compelling and effective Web sites, today announced Macromedia Aria Enterprise 5.0 software, offering Global 2000 companies the most scalable solution for mission-critical Web site tracking, analysis, and reporting. With this release, Aria Enterprise 5.0 software includes comprehensive new reporting capabilities, and a relational architecture based on Oracle 8i, the market standard in enterprise relational databases. These new features increase scalability and enhance the e-marketer’s understanding of online user behavior, marketing promotions, and e-commerce transactions. In a separate announcement today, Macromedia introduced Macromedia LikeMinds 5.0 software, the latest version of its real-time Web personalization solution.

Macromedia Aria Enterprise 5.0 sets the standard for analyzing online business events by collecting the highest value information, moving beyond traditional Web traffic measurement to include marketing campaign results, e-commerce events, and business-to-business metrics. This information allows e-marketers to quickly and easily take action to meet their online business objectives. The solution is also integrated with Macromedia Dreamweaver software to more easily build intelligence for measurement and analysis into content as it is authored.

Macromedia Aria Enterprise 5.0 software meets the comprehensive needs of the online enterprise. Its scalability allows Aria customers to keep up with continually increasing site traffic. An open architecture and Oracle 8i platform allows for integration of e-business data with other enterprise data and leverages industry standard database tools to reduce cost of administration. New support for integration with third party auditing firms provides independent verification of mission-critical online data.

New reporting features in Macromedia Aria Enterprise 5.0 software, such as a Web-based, drag-and-drop custom reporting interface, allows the e-marketer to analyze and report on the information that is critical to their e-business success. Business users can now move beyond basic Web traffic reports to analyze rich, real-time information about what is actually happening with their online business. Aria Enterprise 5.0 software customers will benefit from new reporting capabilities that determine ROI for online advertising and marketing campaigns, site navigation behavior, and content effectiveness, information critical for achieving online business objectives for complex, high-volume Web sites.

"Macromedia Aria Enterprise 5.0 sets the standard for e-businesses to measure the mission-critical effectiveness of their Web sites," said Stephen Elop, senior vice president and general manager for Macromedia. "By tapping into the most advanced forms of Web site analytics, Aria provides the richest, most comprehensive view into the behavior of an online audience, enabling the delivery of an effective visitor experience."

Macromedia Aria Enterprise 5.0 software is an integral component of the Macromedia Web Content Lifecycle, which is Macromedia’s vision for the integration of solutions designed to help customers build, manage, personalize, and dynamic Web content while engaging customers on complex, business-critical Web sites. Macromedia Aria Enterprise 5.0 software integrates with other components of the Web Content Lifecycle; for example, Macromedia Dreamweaver Objects for Aria allow content developers to add measurement and analysis functionality while they are creating content, which in turn can be later tracked by Aria Enterprise 5.0 software, saving companies the expense and disruption of retrofitting sites to measure return on investment.

For more information on Macromedia Aria Enterprise 5.0 software, which is expected to be available in Summer 2000, visit the Web site at www.macromedia.com/software/aria/. To speak to a sales representative, call (800) 700-3282.
