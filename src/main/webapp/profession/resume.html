Experience

Director, Agile Program Office

Citrix Online

Public Company; Computer Software industry

October 2007 – Present (3 years)

At Citrix Online, my team facilitates the efficient and iterative delivery of high-value projects to customers. Our mandate is to promote high-productivity, not only in engineering, where we report, but throughout the company.

Citrix Online uses Scrum to organize most engineering, some marketing, and some customer support work. Scrum focuses on teams of 9 people or less; to make a multi-product agile company, we needed something much bigger.

in 2008Q4, I developed, and now evangelize and manage, a highly agile approach to organize our 200 person engineering and product management work, which we call Enterprise Scrum. It drives agile principles throughout Citrix Online. A paper on this work appears in 2010 IEEE Hawaii Intl Conference on System Science. This system allows us to adapt more rapidly to emerging market conditions than other mid-to-large engineering groups can.

Citrix Online's user experience design and testing group indirectly reports to me. This sophisticated group works iteratively to design, prototype and test user interactions; it seeks simple and highly intuitive interfaces. When people say "I love Citrix Online products, because they are so easy to use," we can credit this group and its people.

Citrix Online uses many continuous improvement techniques: Scrum, XP, Kaizen, Theory of Constraints. We like Net Present Value for prioritizing work. Our group researches, packages and evangelizes these decision-making and organizational processes for Citrix Online.

Citrix Online has highly-scalable, fault-tolerant network-based applications using Java web, infrastructure and streaming components, Flash/Flex, HTML and Javascript clients, C++ clients. We create and refine continuous build, unit testing, integration testing, performance testing and rapid deployment mechanisms all the time.

I was a primary catalyst in transforming Citrix Online to the highly agile company you see today.
