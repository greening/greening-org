    Today I’m going to talk about targeting and personalization in marketing, especially on web pages. One example of web personalization is a new concept we call “many-to-one marketing,” which leverages the power of communities to help you and your customers sell more products.
    The bottom line is that you, web site developers, have a unique opportunity to help your customers make more money. If you can help your customers make more money, they will come back to you again for more help in the future.
