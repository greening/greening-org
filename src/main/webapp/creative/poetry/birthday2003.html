Fast haiku, births January, what the fuck

Haiku written for a birthday dinner Jan 19 2003. Moses Ma, Randy Sugiwara, Dan Greening.

5-7-5 All Rhyming

Moses

Sweaty, naked, stray,
women ride while horses play:
Sex societe.

Randy

Are your chakras right?
Harmonize the dark with light
to find inner might.

Dan

What if thoughts too brief
joined to build a strong belief?
Communal motif!

3-5-3 Two Rhyming Stanzas

Moses

Brain cashless.
Now ambition floats
new tech boats.

Randy

Sweet words flow
to seduce right brains.
Smart maestro.

Dan

Make a wish
then seek forever.
Face bluish.
