100 Word Professional Biography

Dan Greening is Citrix Online's Enterprise ScrumMaster, balancing Product Management priorities with Engineering effort, maximizing transparency, eliminating impediments and promoting continuous improvement. He consulted with Overstock.com to restructure its engineering department, as acting Chief Scientist.

Greening was CEO for BigTribe, a proximity advertising network startup, Engineering VP for Macromedia, providing web analytics software, CTO of LikeMinds, the personalization startup Macromedia acquired, and CEO of Chaco, a virtual reality software startup.

Greening writes on topics related to organization and technology. He was awarded 3 NSF grants for small business innovation. He holds a computer science Ph.D. from UCLA.
